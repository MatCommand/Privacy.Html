Privacy Policy

MatCommand Wrestling
Last Updated: May 2026

MatCommand Wrestling respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how users can manage their data.

Information We Collect

MatCommand Wrestling is designed to function primarily as a local wrestling training, practice planning, and coaching application.

The app does not require users to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information.

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Practice sessions
  • Drill selections
  • Conditioning routines
  • Timer settings
  • App preferences
  • Backup or imported session files created by the user

Information We Do Not Collect

MatCommand Wrestling does not intentionally collect:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Payment information
  • Government identification
  • Precise location data
  • Health or medical records
  • Heart rate, calories, body measurements, or wearable device data

Device Permissions

Depending on the platform and app version, MatCommand Wrestling may use limited device functionality for normal app operation.

  • Storage or file access: Used only for importing, exporting, saving, or restoring user-created practice data and backups.
  • Audio playback: Used only for timer sounds, alerts, and beep functions.

MatCommand Wrestling does not use the camera, microphone recording, contacts, SMS messages, or GPS location.

How We Use Information

Any data created inside the app is used only to provide the app’s features, including building practices, running drills, saving sessions, restoring backups, and maintaining user preferences.

Data Storage

Practice data and app settings are stored locally on the user’s device unless the user chooses to export, back up, or share that information.

MatCommand Wrestling does not operate a cloud storage service for user data.

Data Sharing

MatCommand Wrestling does not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with third parties.

The app does not intentionally use advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or analytics tools to collect user data.

Internet Connectivity

Core app features are designed to work without an internet connection. If future versions add online services, this Privacy Policy will be updated to explain those features.

Children’s Privacy

MatCommand Wrestling may be used by coaches, parents, athletes, wrestling clubs, schools, and sports organizations. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so the issue can be reviewed.

User Control

Users may manage their data by:

  • Deleting saved sessions inside the app
  • Resetting app data
  • Deleting exported backup files
  • Uninstalling the app

Security

Reasonable efforts are made to keep locally stored app data safe. However, users are responsible for securing their own devices and any exported backup files.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any updates will be reflected by changing the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.
